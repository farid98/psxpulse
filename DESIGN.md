# PSXPulse — Design Summary

A mobile-first analytical dashboard for the KSE-100. Four views, accessible via a top nav bar (mobile) or left sidebar (desktop):

**Overview** — Market snapshot: index, volume, breadth, news sentiment. Sector list where each row expands to show the Buy/Hold/Reduce rationale.

**Portfolio** — Holdings table with P&L and portfolio weight. Tap any row to jump straight to that stock's signal analysis.

**Signals** — Single-stock deep-dive. Search any KSE-100 stock, then explore four scored dimensions — Technical, Fundamental, News, and Overall — each as a compact tab.

**Discover** — Two tabs. *Opportunities*: algorithm-ranked picks with an investment thesis for each. *Scanner*: technical screener with preset scans (breakouts, volume spikes, RSI divergence, etc.) shown as pills — tap one to see the qualifying stocks.

---

All data is illustrative mock data. The "Manage Portfolio" button and "+ Custom" scan are placeholders for features not yet built.
